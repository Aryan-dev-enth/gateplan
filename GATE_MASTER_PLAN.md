## Mock Test Strategy (Topper Method)

1. **When to start:** Aug 15 (not before — syllabus first)
2. **Frequency:** 1–2 per week in August, 3+ per week in Sept+
3. **After every mock:**
   - Mark every wrong answer
   - Find the concept gap (not just the answer)
   - Revise that concept same day
   - Re-attempt similar questions next day
4. **Score doesn't matter in August** — pattern recognition does
5. **Never skip analysis** — this is where rank is built

---

## PYQ Strategy (How Toppers Extract Patterns)

- Solve year-wise (2015 → 2024), not topic-wise
- After solving, tag each question: `concept | difficulty | mistake type`
- Revisit wrong ones every 2 weeks
- Notice repeated concepts — those are your high-priority topics
- Last 5 years PYQs = most important, do them 3x minimum
