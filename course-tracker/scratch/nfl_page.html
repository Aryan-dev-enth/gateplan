NFL MT Recruitment 2026 – Apply Online for 21 Posts | Last Date 12 June 2026

Last Updated: May 23, 2026 | Author: Admin

National Fertilizers Limited (NFL), a Navratna Central Public Sector Undertaking under the Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, has invited online applications from eligible Indian Nationals for engagement as Management Trainees in various disciplines for its Units/Offices. A total of 21 vacancies are available in E-1 level on regular basis.

The NFL Management Trainee Recruitment 2026 Online Application process starts from 13th May 2026 and closes on 12th June 2026. This article provides complete information about NFL Management Trainee Vacancy 2026, including post-wise vacancy details, eligibility criteria, pay scale, selection process, official notification PDF, and online application link.

NFL Management Trainee Recruitment 2026

www.indgovtjobs.in

Total Vacancies

21 Posts

18-27 years (as on 30.04.2026)

✅ How to Apply NFL Management Trainee Recruitment 2026?

Eligible candidates must apply online only through the NFL careers portal. The online application steps are as follows:-

  • Step 1: Visit the NFL recruitment portal and register using valid email ID and mobile number.
  • Step 2: Fill the online application form carefully with personal, educational, experience and category details.
  • Step 3: Upload scanned copies of photograph, signature, educational certificates, experience letters (if any) and other required documents.
  • Step 4: Pay the application fee online (if applicable) and submit the form.
  • Step 5: Take a printout of the finally submitted application form for future reference.
